## MAIN PROTAGONISTS

She holds a book in one hand and has a pistol at her side. Background suggests both scholarly pursuits and combat readiness. Alternate history East Asian aesthetic with subtle biotech elements. Soft watercolor washes in greens and purples, bold ink linework.

### 2. Sun (Princess Yi Seong-Ma)
**Prompt:** Pen and ink watercolor of an ambitious young Asian princess with sharp, beautiful features—high cheekbones and almond eyes that burn with intensity. Background hints at palace architecture and political power. Alternate history Korean aesthetic. Watercolor in jewel tones—emerald, gold, deep purple. Confident, commanding presence.

### 3. Sifu Advika Shidao

Expression is serene but alert, like a coiled spring. Background suggests mountain temple or training ground. Muted watercolors—grays, soft purples, green accents. Ink shadows emphasize her mysterious, warrior nature.

### 4. Jack Brand - International Man of Mystery

Background suggests luxury cruise ship or casino. Retro spy aesthetic meets biotech future. Watercolor in cool blues and warm ambers for contrast. Sharp ink lines, sophisticated composition.

## STYLE NOTES FOR ALL PROMPTS:
- Pen and ink watercolor technique: bold black ink linework with loose, expressive watercolor washes
- Alternate history aesthetic: 1960s-2020s fusion with biotech elements
- Color palettes emphasize the story's dark biotech atmosphere—deep purples, toxic greens, blood reds, clinical whites, natural earth tones
- Eastern and Global South aesthetics dominate over Western
- Technology is organic-mechanical hybrid, bio-integrated
- Post-war world: beautiful but wounded, practical but hopeful
- Each piece should feel like concept art for a prestige sci-fi drama
